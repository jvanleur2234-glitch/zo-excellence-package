## The Vision

Every small business needs AI employees but can't afford to build them from scratch. Solomon OS is the software platform that lets any agency, freelancer, or consultant sell AI employee services to their clients — without needing to code.

**Analogy:** WordPress for AI agents. Not "we host your AI." Not "we build your AI." But "here's the software that IS your AI employee, hosted where YOU want, sold through who YOU trust."

**Addressable market:** 30M+ businesses in the US. Every vertical. Every size. Every industry.

## The Four Business Models (Stacking)

### Model 1 — Consulting / First Dollars (NOW)
**What:** You personally build and manage AI agents for select clients.  
**Who pays:** $99-499/mo per agent, per client.  
**Who:** Jack Vanleur (RE), anyone Joseph personally knows.  
**Revenue ceiling:** $10-50K/mo (10-20 clients × 5 agents × $200).  
**Effort:** High. You're doing the build.  
**Status:** START HERE.

### Model 2 — Agency License (3-6 months)  
**What:** Agencies (marketing, RE, legal, medical, etc.) white-label Solomon OS to serve their clients.  
**What they pay:** $200/mo per agency + $10/client/month (portal fee).  
**What you provide:** Training, templates, support, co-branding.  
**Revenue ceiling:** $500K/mo (100 agencies × $200 + 5,000 clients × $10).  
**Effort:** Medium. You're supporting agencies, not individual businesses.  
**Why agencies:** They already have clients. They just need better AI tools.  

### Model 3 — Enterprise License (6-12 months)
**What:** Larger companies license Solomon OS for internal use.  
**What they pay:** $2,000-10,000/mo.  
**What you provide:** Custom agents, dedicated support, SLAs.  
**Revenue ceiling:** $5M+/mo.  
**Effort:** High. Enterprise sales cycle is long.  

### Model 4 — Platform / Franchise (Year 2+)
**What:** Solomon OS becomes the standard. Independent consultants worldwide buy licenses.  
**What they pay:** $99/mo per consultant + revenue share on client fees.  
**Revenue ceiling:** Unlimited. 10,000 consultants × $99 = $1M/mo base + revenue share.  
**Leverage:** You don't touch clients. Your licensees do.  
**This is the billion dollar model.**
